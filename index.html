Technoscape18

Need

Almost every naturally occurring element or compound is found in an impure state. Often these raw materials must be separated and purified before they can be put to productive use making separation processes essential to the modern industrial economy.

Demand

The increased demand for “ultra-pure” material has shifted the focus towards new separation processes thereby giving an impetus to its advancement.



Solution

In pursuit of this, the Department of Chemical Engineering, VIT is convening Technoscape18, an International Conference on Separation Technologies in Chemical, Biochemical, Petroleum and Environmental Engineering.

Objective

The conference aims to provide an interactive knowledge sharing platform for experts, researchers, engineers, scientists and industrialists working in the field of separation science and technology from around the globe.


About Us

VIT University


Founded in 1984, as Vellore Engineering College, the Institute was upgraded as a University in recognition of its academic excellence in 2001. The institution was founded by the North Arcot Educational Trust under the chairmanship of the present Chancellor Dr. G. Viswanathan. The university has a student strength of 24,000 with more than 1400 faculty members. It has 19 Undergraduate and 34 Postgraduate programmes as well as research programmes including Ph.D.

Department of Chemical Engineering


Established in 1994, The Department of Chemical Engineering,VIT University is one of the oldest departments. The faculties of the deparment bring to the university the experiences they have garnered from institutes such as Indian Institute of Technology, the Indian Institute of Science and other premier institutes with expertise in various areas of specialization. They are actively involved in sponsored research, consultancy work and collaborative R&D projects with various regional and national industries and government agencies. Apart from this, the department also liaises with CO2 Research facility which caters to green technological projects like better utilization of carbon dioxide to meet the global need for renewable fuels using agro materials as feed stock.

About Vellore


Vellore, also called Fort City, is located on the banks of the Palar River in the north eastern part of Tamil Nadu and boasts a rich history and cultural heritage. The city is home to Christian Medical College and VIT University, both of which are among the top ten Institutions in their respective fields. It is also a major centre for medical tourism in India. The Vellore region is the top exporter of leather goods in the country and also home to several manufacturing and automobile companies. The city offers great tourist spots such as Vellore Fort, Government Museum, Science Park, and Religious destinations such as Jalakandeshwar Temple and Srilakshmi Golden Temple. Other places of interest near Vellore include hill stations like Yercaud and Yelagiri as well as Kanchipuram and Mahabalipuram, famed for their architectural magnificence.
